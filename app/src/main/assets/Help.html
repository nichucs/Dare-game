Dare Game

Overview

This is a simple game, that is played by a group of people. At least 3 people are needed to play this game. It’s a simplified version of Truth/dare game.

How to play

  1. Assign each player a number: You can assign by order, or assign randomly. The objective is, if there are 8 people, all should have assigned a number, starting from 1 to 8.
  2. Enter the player count in the first screen and click on start button: This is needed to assign each player a task
  3. Check the lucky person to perform: Click on the button, ‘Who’s next’
  4. Check the action for him to do: Click on the button, ‘What to do’
  5. Repeat steps 3 & 4 .

Add/ Remove dare actions

You can Add / Remove / Edit your dare actions in the settings. Use the button to add, and swipe the item you want to delete or edit.
